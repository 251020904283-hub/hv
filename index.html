Welcome to Phenikaa University

Phenikaa University is one of the leading private universities in Vietnam. The university provides high-quality education, modern facilities, and opportunities for students to develop academic knowledge and practical skills.

Located in Hanoi, Phenikaa University offers a dynamic learning environment, advanced laboratories, experienced lecturers, and international cooperation programs. Students can participate in research projects, clubs, sports, and volunteer activities throughout their studies.
